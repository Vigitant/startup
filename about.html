Quintus is a game that I've been playing for years - either scribbled in a notebook, on a note-taking app in my phone, or just in my head.
When I was told that the final project for my web development course would be one of my own choosing, I knew immediately that I wanted to create a self-contained version of my game.

Many thanks go out to my professor, Lee Jensen, and his assistants.

To see the code powering this site, check out the gitHub repository.

To see more about me, check out my profile.

Thanks for playing.
